Shipping Information

If your order is above $25 in value, you are eligible for free shipping. If not, the shipping rate is $10/KG, which will depend on the package weight. The package is transported by CouriersPlease and is insured up to $3000. The usual package transit time is 5 Business Days, but it may sometimes be longer, and the shipment is available to track as soon as it is handed to the courier.
